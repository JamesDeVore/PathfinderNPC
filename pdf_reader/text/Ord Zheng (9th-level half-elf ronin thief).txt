SPECIAL ABILITIES 
Armor Training (Ex) When wearing armor, you reduce your armor check penalty by 2 (to a minimum of 0) 
and increase your armor’s maximum Dexterity modifier limit by 2. In addition, your speed is not 
reduced for wearing medium or heavy armor. 
Bravery (Ex) You gain a +3 bonus on Will saves against mind-affecting effects (included above). 
Reflexive Improvisation You gain a +2 racial bonus on untrained skill checks. 
Weapon Training (Ex) You receive a +4 bonus to attack and damage rolls when using heavy blades, to any 
combat maneuver checks made with heavy blades, and to your Combat Maneuver Defense when 
defending against disarm and sunder attempts made against heavy blades.
